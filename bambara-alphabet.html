Bambara Alphabet & Pronunciation

The Bambara alphabet uses Latin letters with specific sounds. Learning the alphabet is the first step to reading and writing Bambara correctly.

Bambara Letters

A, B, C, D, E, Ɛ, F, G, H, I, J, K, L, M, N, Ɲ, Ŋ, O, Ɔ, P, R, S, T, U, W, Y, Z

Vowels and Consonants

Bambara has 7 vowels and clear consonant sounds. Pronunciation is regular and easy for beginners.
