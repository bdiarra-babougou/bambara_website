Basic Bambara Conversation

Greeting Example

Ousmane: I ni ce! Hakɛto, e tɔgɔ?
Binta: Unse! Ne tɔgɔ ye Binta.
Ousmane: Ne tɔgɔ ye Ousmane. Binta jamu?
Binta: Ne jamu ko Diarra. E dun Ousmane?

Why Practice Conversation?

Conversation is the fastest way to learn Bambara. My lessons focus on real dialogue used by native speakers.
